Rules of Chat

  • Be respectful to the streamer, mods, and other viewers.
  • Do not post walls of text.
  • Do not post NSFW (gore/porn/extreme imagery).
  • Do not post sexist or racist comments.
  • Do not share personal info, including your own.
  • No self-promotion or advertisement of other streamers.
  • No backseating unless asked for.
  • If you have an issue with another viewer or a mod, take it to whispers.

Keeping it Fun

The best way to have an enjoyable experience is to follow the rules above. Anyone who does not adhere to the rules will receive anywhere from a warning to a permanent ban depending on the severity of the infraction and/or number of infractions. Please be respectful with us as we will be respectful towards you.

Unban

If you are banned, you can appeal yourself by just being honest and communicating respectfully to the mods or myself. You can message an admin or Somespace on the Discord; please keep it concise.
